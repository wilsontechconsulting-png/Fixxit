Common Plumbing Warning Signs

Don’t ignore these red flags — catching problems early can save thousands in water damage repair costs:

  • Water stains on ceilings or walls
  • Low water pressure throughout your home
  • Slow or gurgling drains
  • Sewage smell inside or outside the house
  • Discolored or rusty tap water
  • Your water bill has increased without a change in habits
  • Age of pipes — older homes may have galvanized or lead pipes that need replacement
