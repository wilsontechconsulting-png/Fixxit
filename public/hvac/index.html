Signs You Need HVAC Service

Not sure if you need a professional? Here are some common signs it’s time to call:

  • Your AC or furnace is not reaching the set temperature
  • Unusual noises (banging, grinding, squealing) from your system
  • Noticeably higher energy bills without a change in usage
  • Inconsistent temperatures from room to room
  • Your system is over 10-15 years old and needs inspection
  • You smell burning or musty odors from vents
  • Your system is cycling on and off too frequently

Don’t wait until the peak of summer or a winter cold snap. Schedule preventive maintenance today.
