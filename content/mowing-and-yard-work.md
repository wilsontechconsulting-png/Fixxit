## Seasonal Lawn Care Tips

**Spring:** Core aeration, overseeding bare spots, apply pre-emergent weed control, start mowing when grass reaches 3-4 inches.

**Summer:** Mow regularly (every 1-2 weeks), water deeply 1-2 times per week, avoid cutting more than 1/3 of blade height at once.

**Fall:** Continue mowing until the first frost, rake leaves promptly, apply fall fertilizer to strengthen roots.

**Winter:** Keep lawn clear of heavy debris, avoid walking on frozen grass, plan for spring improvements.
